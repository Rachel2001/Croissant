Croissants, the flaky and buttery pastries originating from France, have become an internationally beloved treat. Characterized by their crescent shape and delicate layers, croissants are made from a laminated dough that involves multiple folds of butter between layers of dough. This process results in a pastry with a light, airy interior and a crisp, golden-brown exterior. The rich and buttery flavor profile, combined with the irresistible texture, makes croissants a popular choice for breakfast or as a delightful accompaniment to coffee or tea. Whether enjoyed plain, filled with chocolate, or layered with ham and cheese, the humble croissant continues to captivate taste buds around the world, transcending cultural boundaries and bringing a touch of indulgence to daily rituals.
